Hobby

I enjoy reading, watching anime, and learning about philosophy. In my free time, I love diving into stories—whether it’s through books, anime, or big questions about life. Reading helps me slow down and reflect; there’s something special about getting lost in a good novel or even a strange, thought-provoking piece of philosophy. Anime brings a different kind of joy—visually beautiful, emotionally rich, and full of imagination. And when I’m in the mood to think deeply, I turn to philosophy. It challenges how I see the world and helps me understand myself a little better. These hobbies are more than just pastimes, they're how I connect, grow, and recharge.
